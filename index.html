MENU:

E-FOOD's menu boasts popular Kenyan appetizers like samosas, mshikaki skewers, and kachumbari salad, extensive entrees including Nyama Choma, Pilau rice, and Githeri stew, and vegetarian options. For dessert, choose from Mandazi, Kaimati, or Mutura. Wash it down with Kenyan tea, Masala chai, mango juice, or Tusker Beer. E-FOOD satisfies cravings with delicious Kenyan cuisine.

Appetizers:

Samosas - Crispy pastry filled with spiced vegetables or meat.

Mshikaki - Skewered meat, usually chicken or beef, marinated in a spicy sauce.

Kachumbari - Tomato and onion salad with lemon and chili peppers.

Mahamri - Sweet fried bread made with coconut milk and served with hot tea.

Entrees:

Nyama Choma - Grilled or roasted meat, often goat or beef, served with ugali, kachumbari, and chili sauce.

Pilau - Spiced rice dish made with cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, served with beef or chicken.

Githeri - Hearty bean and corn stew made with kidney beans and maize.

Sukuma Wiki - Collard greens sautéed with onions and tomatoes, often served with ugali or rice.

Irio - Mashed potato dish mixed with green peas, corn, and onions.

Desserts:

Mandazi - Sweet fried bread made with flour, coconut milk, and sugar, often served with hot tea.

Kaimati - Sweet dumplings made with flour, sugar, and cardamom, served with honey syrup.

Mutura - Grilled sausage made with beef or goat meat, often spiced with coriander and chili peppers.

Beverages:

Kenyan Tea - Strong, sweet tea made with milk and spices like ginger and cardamom.

Tusker Beer - Popular Kenyan beer made from locally sourced ingredients.

Mango Juice - Freshly squeezed juice made from sweet Kenyan mangoes.

Masala Chai - Spiced tea made with milk, cinnamon, cardamom.
